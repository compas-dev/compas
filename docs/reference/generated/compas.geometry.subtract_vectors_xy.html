subtract_vectors_xy

compas.geometry.subtract_vectors_xy(u, v)[source]

Subtract one vector from another, assuming they lie in the XY plane.

Parameters:
  • u (sequence of float) – The XY(Z) coordinates of the first vector.
  • v (sequence of float) – The XY(Z) coordinates of the second vector.
Returns:

tuple – Resulting vector in the XY-plane (Z = 0.0)

Examples

>>>
subtract_vectors sum_vectors
