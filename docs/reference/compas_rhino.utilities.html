objects

Note

Note that the meaning of the naming conventions used here is somewhat different than those used in Rhino and rhinoscriptsyntax modules.

Functions prefixed with get_ imply that guids are obtained by applying some kind of filter. For example, get all lines with a specified naming pattern, on a specific layer, or with a specific color.

Functions prefixed with select_ imply that guids are obtained by manual selection.
